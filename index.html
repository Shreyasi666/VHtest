Villy Hit Entertainment

Villy Hit Entertainment, formally called VH Entertainment is an agency founded by a group of people in 2020. VH recruits members and trains them to their full potential. We mainly focus on rap, dance and vocals. After training the official members are asked to do various covers on vocal and dance in different songs, said covers are posted on our official YouTube Channel. Members are also assigned to different groups in which they perform in. Group members work together to produce and compose songs. There is a lot more to do in VH and even YOU can join in three simple steps, scroll down to know more. Looking forward in working with you.

How do I get into VH?



Getting into VH Entertainment couldn't have been easier! The process of getting in is very direct and simple, and the only thing we require is skill! To get started you will need to make 3 different videos, the first on dancing, the second on rapping and the third, on singing. For more information about our requirements you may visit the page in the given link. You will need to send these videos on our official Instagram account or E-mail. You will get a reply from our agents in less than 48 hours.
Congrats! You have finished the first step of getting into VH!
If you have done everything correctly and sent all three videos, you will be given a form by us and requested to fill it out in which you will fill out your personal details. If you are worried about privacy, there is no need. No personal information is given to anyone outside VH. Your details will be safe and secure.
The third and last step is simply, waiting! If all the details are filled out correctly and there is no problem, you will be contacted shortly in a matter of days.
